## Open Elective (3 Credits)

1 course from any department or from the trail courses list.

---

## Internship / Research or BIO103L (1 Credit — choose one)

- **Option A — CSE498R** (Internship, 320+ hours) or **CSE498I** (Research with faculty)
- **Option B — BIO103L** (Biology lab from GED core)

Only the chosen option counts; the other is excluded.
